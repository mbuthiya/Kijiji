Payment features

Request and track payments

With Kijiji you can easily request payment from your tenants through the platform. You can then at a glance see which payments are pending and follow up.

Split late payments in installments

We understand that some times your tenants might not be able to make payments in full. So Kijiji allows you to split a payment to be paid in easy installments.

Record income and expenses

Kijiji helps you store a transparent record of your income and expenses. This provides you a clear picture of how your rental business is performing.
